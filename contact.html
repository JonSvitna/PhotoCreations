Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and 2-4 weeks for other services, though we can often accommodate shorter notice based on availability.

What's included in your packages?

Each package includes professional photography, high-resolution edited images, and an online gallery. Specific inclusions vary by service type.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We're happy to travel for your photography needs. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our service area.

How long until I receive my photos?

Turnaround time varies by service: 2-3 weeks for portraits, 4-6 weeks for weddings, and 1-2 weeks for commercial projects.

Can I request specific shots?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share any specific shots or styles you'd like to see. We'll work with you to create a shot list.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for rescheduling. Deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a future booking within one year.
